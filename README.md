# 📊 Business Insights & Data-Driven Decisions

## Executive Summary

Customer churn is one of the most significant challenges faced by subscription-based businesses because acquiring a new customer is considerably more expensive than retaining an existing one. This project develops a machine learning solution to identify customers who are likely to churn, enabling proactive retention strategies.

After comparing multiple machine learning algorithms, **XGBoost** achieved the best predictive performance and was selected as the final production model. The project also incorporates **SHAP Explainability** to provide transparent and interpretable predictions for business stakeholders.

---

# Dataset Overview

| Attribute | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| Domain | Telecommunications |
| Prediction Type | Binary Classification |
| Target Variable | **Churn Value** |
| Business Problem | Predict whether a customer will leave the company |
| Algorithms Evaluated | Logistic Regression, Random Forest, XGBoost, LightGBM |

---

# Data Quality Assessment

Several preprocessing techniques were applied to improve data quality before model training.

### Data Cleaning

- Removed customer identifiers that do not contribute to prediction.
- Eliminated target leakage variables including:
  - Churn Label
  - Churn Score
  - CLTV
  - Churn Reason
- Converted **Total Charges** into numeric format.
- Removed records containing invalid or missing values.
- Standardized categorical values such as:
  - No internet service → No
  - No phone service → No

---

# Feature Engineering

Instead of relying only on raw features, additional business-oriented features were created.

### Tenure Group

Customers were segmented based on their relationship duration.

- 0–1 Year
- 1–2 Years
- 2–4 Years
- 4+ Years

**Business Value**

Different customer lifecycle stages exhibit different churn behavior, making tenure segmentation highly valuable.

---

### Value Score

```
Value Score = Monthly Charges × Tenure Months
```

This feature estimates the long-term value generated by each customer.

**Business Value**

High-value customers should receive priority retention efforts.

---

### Total Services

A new feature was created representing the number of services subscribed by each customer.

**Business Value**

Customer engagement can be measured more effectively than evaluating individual services independently.

---

# Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

The exploratory analysis focused on identifying customer characteristics associated with higher churn rates.

## Contract Type

### Observation

Customers with **Month-to-Month contracts** exhibit the highest churn rate.

### Business Insight

Short-term contracts provide customers with greater flexibility to leave competitors.

### Business Decision

- Promote annual contracts
- Provide renewal discounts
- Introduce loyalty rewards

---

## Customer Tenure

### Observation

Customers with shorter tenure are considerably more likely to churn.

### Business Insight

The first year of the customer lifecycle is the most critical retention period.

### Business Decision

- Improve onboarding experience
- Launch welcome campaigns
- Increase engagement during the first 12 months

---

## Monthly Charges

### Observation

Customers paying higher monthly charges demonstrate higher churn probability.

### Business Insight

Customers become increasingly price-sensitive as monthly costs rise.

### Business Decision

- Offer personalized pricing
- Bundle services
- Introduce flexible subscription plans

---

## Internet Service

### Observation

Internet service type influences customer churn behavior.

### Business Insight

Service quality and reliability significantly affect customer satisfaction.

### Business Decision

- Improve service reliability
- Optimize network performance
- Address customer complaints proactively

---

## Service Adoption

Customers subscribed to additional services such as:

- Online Security
- Device Protection
- Tech Support
- Online Backup

generally show lower churn tendencies.

### Business Insight

Higher customer engagement improves loyalty.

### Business Decision

Promote bundled service packages instead of selling services individually.

# Model Development

The following machine learning models were evaluated.

| Model | Purpose |
|--------|----------|
| Logistic Regression | Baseline linear model |
| Random Forest | Ensemble learning |
| LightGBM | Gradient boosting |
| **XGBoost** | Final production model |

Model evaluation was performed using:

- ROC-AUC
- Classification Report
- Confusion Matrix
- ROC Curve

A **Stratified Train-Test Split** was used to preserve class distribution.

---

# Why XGBoost?

XGBoost delivered the strongest predictive performance among all evaluated models.

### Advantages

- Excellent ROC-AUC
- Robust handling of class imbalance
- Strong generalization capability
- Reduced overfitting through boosting
- High production reliability

Therefore, XGBoost was selected as the final deployment model.

---

# Model Explainability

One of the major objectives of this project was building an explainable AI solution rather than a black-box model.

SHAP (SHapley Additive exPlanations) was used to explain every prediction.

The project includes:

- Global Feature Importance
- SHAP Beeswarm Plot
- Individual Customer Waterfall Plot

This enables business teams to understand **why** a customer is predicted to churn instead of simply knowing **that** they might churn.

# Key Business Insights

## 1. Month-to-Month Customers Have Highest Churn Risk

Customers without long-term commitments are significantly more likely to leave.

### Recommendation

- Offer annual subscription discounts.
- Provide loyalty incentives.
- Introduce contract renewal campaigns.

---

## 2. New Customers Require Early Attention

Customers in their first year have the highest probability of churn.

### Recommendation

- Strengthen onboarding programs.
- Schedule proactive follow-ups.
- Deliver personalized welcome offers.

---

## 3. Higher Monthly Charges Increase Churn Risk

Price sensitivity is a major driver of customer attrition.

### Recommendation

- Introduce customized pricing.
- Bundle premium services.
- Offer retention discounts to high-risk customers.

---

## 4. Customers with More Services Are More Loyal

Customers using multiple services are generally less likely to churn.

### Recommendation

- Promote cross-selling.
- Create bundled service packages.
- Encourage adoption of value-added services.

---

## 5. Customer Lifetime Value Matters

High-value customers represent greater financial risk if they churn.

### Recommendation

Develop VIP retention strategies specifically for high-value customers.
